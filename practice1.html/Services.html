Restaurant Services
PepsiCo provides beverages and snack foods to numerous restaurants, fast-food chains, and hospitality establishments through its foodservice division. This includes offering fountain drinks, bottled beverages, and snack products.
Health and Wellnes intiatives
PepsiCo offers products that cater to health-conscious consumers, including low-calorie beverages, zero-sugar drinks, and a variety of nutritious snacks. They are also involved in initiatives to reduce added sugars, sodium, and saturated fats in their products.
Sustainability and Environmental intiatives
PepsiCo is engaged in various sustainability efforts, including recycling programs, water conservation initiatives, and sustainable farming practices. They aim to reduce their environmental footprint through packaging innovations, waste reduction, and energy efficiency improvements.
Corporate partnerships and sponsers
PepsiCo is known for its high-profile sponsorships in sports, music, and entertainment. This includes partnerships with major sports leagues like the NFL, NBA, and UEFA, as well as music festivals and events.
Marketing and Advertizing
PepsiCo provides extensive marketing and advertising support for its brands. This includes digital marketing, traditional media advertising, and experiential marketing through events and promotions.
Community and Social
PepsiCo is involved in various community outreach and social responsibility programs, such as educational initiatives, disaster relief efforts, and support for local communities.
